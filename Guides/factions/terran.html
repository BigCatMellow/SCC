Race dossier

Terran

Combined-arms, infrastructure-first, and flexible. Terran faction and tactical cards read like doctrine, logistics, and battlefield tools that let otherwise honest units punch above their baseline.
How to read this section
When a Terran card looks simple, check whether it is quietly changing the action economy or the reserve / disengage pressure around a fight.
Included here
What this race section contains
  • 2 faction cards
  • 10 tactical cards
  • 7 unit cards
Watch for
Likely rules friction

Terran cards frequently interact with PLACE, command-point spending, repair, and initiative manipulation. Those are powerful because they alter tempo, not because they always add raw stats.
