Race dossier

Protoss

Elite, precise, and timing-sensitive. The Protoss files lean hard on positioning tricks, extra activations, protective reactions, and infrastructure cards that turn resource spending into clean tempo swings.
How to read this section
Read Protoss entries with one eye on timing and one eye on board position. A lot of their power is not raw damage; it is activation control, placement, and selective force multiplication.

Watch for
Likely rules friction

The main rules friction here is resource naming. The faction files visually present Protoss resource as EN, while the uploaded core rules name Protoss resource as PE. This companion preserves the card presentation but flags the discrepancy wherever it matters.
