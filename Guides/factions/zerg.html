Race dossier

Zerg

Pressure, creep, recursion, and battlefield bodies. Zerg files contain more swarm pieces, more conditional buffs, and more effects that care about state changes like BURROWED, ON CREEP, or sudden board presence.
How to read this section
Zerg cards often look explosive. The safe reading method is to ask what state the target was in before the effect, what state it is in after the effect, and what restrictions still continue to apply.

Watch for
Likely rules friction

Zerg text creates a lot of questions about timing, placement, and whether an effect is creating a new unit, restoring a position, or just changing a status. Read those cards against PLACE, reserves, and keyword timing.
