Faction field guide

A plain-English army guide for Protoss, Terran, and Zerg that matches the philosophy of the core rules guide: readable first, selective RAW where wording friction is likely, and practical notes where actual play questions tend to arise.
Scope
This book covers faction cards, tactical cards, and units. It does not replace the core rulebook. It explains how to read faction material cleanly and how to connect it back to the rules engine behind it.

How to read an entry
Step 1

Plain-English reading: what the card is really doing without assuming the reader already speaks in rules jargon.

Step 2

Role / strengths / watch-outs: how the card behaves on the table, what it wants, and where it can misfire.

Step 3

Synergy and matchup watch: how the card behaves inside a list and what to be careful of against each race.

Step 4

RAW watch: only when wording, timing, placement, or exceptions are likely to create real table friction.
